Fred Rogers

20 Mar 1928 - 27 Feb 2003

When I was growing up, Mr. Rogers' Neighborhood was a constant in my world. I felt like I was too old for puppets and make believe, but I was young enough to be enamored with some of the things he would show his viewers, like a crayon factory.

As I grew older, I came to appreciate the subtle way he helped me keep from walking down a different path. I've come to understand why Fred was the way he was, and how he grew up. Fred Rogers' neighborhood is my neighborhood. And it's your neighborhood, too.
